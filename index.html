About Me

Full-stack web developer originally from Portland, ME, I graduated from UMaine Farmington in 2011 with a B.A. in Geography. After college I moved to the Western U.S. to pursue skiing and river running, landing in Silverton Colorado, Moab Utah, and eventually Salt Lake City Utah where I fell in love with the Wasatch Mountains. I worked as a full time Ski Patroller in the Central Wasatch Mountains for 5 winters at the Canyons and then Snowbird. I am currently waiting tables in SLC and spending my time pursuing Backcountry Skiing, Whitewater Kayaking, and Trail Running.
I have blended my passions for outdoor adventure sports, and Geography into a unique approach to web development with a focus on creating geospatial web applications which enhance a users knowledge and enjoyment while enjoying the outdoors.
