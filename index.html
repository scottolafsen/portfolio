About Me

I am 32 year old Native of Portland, ME. I graduated from UMaine Farmington in 2010 with a B.A. in Geography. After college I moved west to pursue skiing, landing in Silverton, CO, then Moab, UT and eventually SLC UT where i fell in love with the area. I worked as a full time Ski Patroller in the Wasatch for 5 winters at the Canyons and then Snowbird. I am currently waiting tables in SLC and spending my time pursuing Backcountry Skiing, Whitewater Kayaking, and Running. Upon graduating from coding bootcamp my hope is to get a job doing Geospatial web/app development, trying to blend my passions of the Outdoors and Geography into Programming.
